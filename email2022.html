Endowment

This year, we are starting to raise money to start an endowment to keep us going strong for years to come. An endowed account represents a long-term investment by our community in the future educational and programming endeavors of Case Western Reserve University’s campus radio station. By donating to the endowment, you will help us have more community events and shows well into the future. Please note that donations to an endowed account cannot be made to any one particular show, and are not eligible for premiums.

So come say hi to your favorite programmers and fellow listeners and enjoy some snacks and drinks on us as a thank you.
